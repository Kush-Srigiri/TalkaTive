Gameplay

How to Play TalkaTive:

Use your voice to control the game! An object will move from the right side of the screen and bounce between the left and center.

Your task: Say the correct word (e.g., the name of the object you see) before it crashes into the left edge. If your answer is wrong or you say nothing, you'll crash into the wall!
